Jimmy

Acerca de mi

Jimmy Lightning is a brown gopher with slightly bigger front teeth. He's almost always depicted wearing a yellow cap. In Peggle 2, his design drastically changed with a darker colour palette, a black ear reamer and a lightning bolt tattoo. In Peggle Blast, his whimsical look from the first games returns, with the only changes being an acorn inside his mouth and a green shirt with a yellow lightining bolt stamp.

Servicios

Multibola

It spawns a second ball immediately after the player hits a green peg.

Special Style Points

  • Triple Play (25,000 pts) - Hitting 2 green pegs in a single turn.

  • Free Ball Frenzy (100,000 pts) - Getting more than 2 balls into the Free Ball Bucket.

  • Multiball Madness (25,000 pts) - Hitting the green peg before hitting the last orange peg.
